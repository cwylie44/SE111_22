Review

Cody Wylie 2/3/2022

As someone that doesn't watch many movies, this movie was an excellent watch. It had plenty of action and made you actually feel a connection to the characters, unlike many movies. The actors also did phenomenal jobs at filing the characters they were playing and certainly added to the atmosphere of the film.

The run time being a little on the longer time really didn't reflect so. The movie was so engaging it flew by while fitting in a lot of overall content. The effects throughout the movie also were on point as there was nothing done wrong that jumped out at you.

I personally would give The Dark Knight a 10/10.
