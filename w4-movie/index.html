This is a batman movie, specifically The Dark Knight. It was released in 2008 and was perceived well by critics as well as the audience. Its set a few years after Batman Begins, as crime is beginning to be contained, an evil mastermind, Joker, appears. This in turn spurs even more crime and Batman has to confront and conquer more than just that in this film.
